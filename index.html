Core Features

Geo-Based Routing

Route53 Geo DNS automatically directs users to the nearest POP for minimal latency and maximum speed.

Edge Cache with Instant Hits

Content cached at the edge delivers instant responses, reducing load times and bandwidth costs.

Origin Fallback Safety
